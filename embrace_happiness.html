EMBRACE HAPPINESS

EMBRACE HAPPINESS IS A COMING-OF-AGE DRAMEDY THAT EXPLORES FRIENDSHIP, CREATIVITY, AND SELF-WORTH IN THE AGE OF CLOUT. TOLD THROUGH THE PERSPECTIVE OF DEVON AND CARTER, TWO TEENS CREATING MUSIC IN BEDROOMS AND CLASSROOMS, THE SHOW BALANCES GROUNDED TEENAGE STRUGGLES WITH SURREAL COMEDIC BEATS AND MODERN INTERNET CULTURE. EACH EPISODE BUILDS UPON THEIR JOURNEY NAVIGATING FAME, SCHOOL, FAMILY, RELATIONSHIPS, AND THE ETHICS OF THE MUSIC WORLD.
